URLTeens, Social Media and Technology 2024
Author(s)Michelle Faverio and Olivia Sidoti
AbstractThis article written by the Pew Research Center shows and analyzes various statistics about the relationships between teens, social media, and technology in 2024. The Pew Research Center acquired these statistics from a large online survey they conducted on 1391 teenagers in 2024. The report compares digital platforms such as Youtube, TikTok, Instagram, and Snapchat and how popular they are to teenagers. The report also analyzed how much time teenagers spend on social media and analyses based on factors on gender, race and ethnicity, age, household income, and partisanship. This report is informative and especially when it is compared to similar previous and future reports.

URLEverything you need to know about social media algorithms
Author(s)Dorcas Adisa
AbstractIn this webblog written on the sproutsocial website the author Dorcas Adisa writes about social media algorithms. This guide goes in depth about many topics that range from defining social media algorithms to mastering algorithms. The guide describes various reasons why social media is important and how they work. The guide also gives detailed examples of algorithm examples. Additionally. The guide explains algorithms from different social media platforms like X, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, Pinterest, and Tumblr. The guide also gives tips for connecting with algorithms and optimizing your platforms and how AI influences the social media algorithms after they are implemented into different apps.
